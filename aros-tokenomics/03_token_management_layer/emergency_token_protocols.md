# Emergency Token Protocols

Emergency protocols provide rapid response mechanisms for monetary incidents.

## Trigger Scenarios

- Major security breach impacting vault balances.
- Regulatory orders requiring immediate action.
- Market manipulation causing severe volatility.
- Critical infrastructure failure in bridges or NodeChain.

## Response Steps

1. **Detection & Escalation**: AI agents or human operators raise alerts routed to the Supervisory
Layer and Governance Council.
2. **Assessment**: Cross-functional response team evaluates impact and proposes countermeasures.
3. **Action**: Possible actions include freezing vaults, pausing issuance, initiating emergency burns,
or deploying insurance reserves.
4. **Communication**: Stakeholders and regulators informed through predefined channels.
5. **Review**: After stabilisation, a post-incident review produces remediation tasks.

## Authority Model

- **Supervisory Override**: Can enact temporary freezes and burns with immediate effect.
- **Governance Ratification**: Any action lasting more than 72 hours requires formal governance vote.
- **Transparency**: Actions logged in audit trail with root cause summaries and future prevention plans.
