# Token Audit Trail

The audit trail ensures every token movement is traceable, reconcilable, and compliant with regulatory
requirements.

## Data Captured

- Transaction metadata (hashes, timestamps, initiating entity).
- Policy identifiers (distribution schedule, burn trigger, emergency action).
- Compliance references (KYC case IDs, reporting obligations).
- Signatures from governance and supervisory approvers when required.

## Storage

- **On-Chain**: Critical events recorded in the decentralized transaction encoding layer.
- **Off-Chain**: Detailed metadata stored in tamper-evident data lakes accessible to auditors.
- **Retention**: Data retained for at least ten years to comply with financial regulations.

## Access Controls

- Role-based access ensures only authorised auditors and governance members can view sensitive data.
- All access is logged, with AI agents scanning for unusual patterns.

## Reporting

Automated pipelines generate monthly, quarterly, and annual reports covering supply, distribution,
locks, and burns. Reports align with international accounting standards and are shareable with partner
institutions.
