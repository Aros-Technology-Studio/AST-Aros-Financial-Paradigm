# Token Supply Governance

Governance ensures supply-side decisions align with long-term economic strategy and regulatory
commitments.

## Governance Bodies

- **Monetary Policy Council**: Sets long-term emission and burn targets.
- **Compliance Committee**: Verifies decisions align with legal requirements.
- **Community Assembly**: Provides advisory votes on strategic initiatives.

## Decision Process

1. **Proposal Submission**: Supply adjustments start as proposals with quantitative justification.
2. **Risk Review**: Economic simulation and threat modelling teams provide impact analysis.
3. **Voting**: Weighted voting occurs per the governance layer rules, incorporating stake and
compliance adjustments.
4. **Implementation**: Approved proposals feed into the Coin and Token Management engines via
versioned configuration updates.

## Controls

- **Time Locks**: Implementation delayed by minimum 72 hours to allow veto or review.
- **Audit Trails**: All deliberations documented and cross-referenced with final actions.
- **Performance Monitoring**: KPIs tracked to ensure outcomes match forecasts; deviations trigger
follow-up proposals.
