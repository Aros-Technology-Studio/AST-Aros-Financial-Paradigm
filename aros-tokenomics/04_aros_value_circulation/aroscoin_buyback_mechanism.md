# ArosCoin Buy-Back Mechanism

Buy-backs stabilise price and reduce supply during low-demand periods.

## Trigger Conditions

- Circulating supply exceeds policy threshold.
- Velocity falls below target band.
- Market price drops below intrinsic value benchmarks.

## Execution Steps

1. **Approval**: Supervisory recommendation reviewed by Monetary Council.
2. **Funding**: Treasury allocates reserve assets for purchase.
3. **Execution**: Programmatic trades executed across approved venues with anti-front-running guards.
4. **Settlement**: Purchased ARO transferred to burn address or reserve vault.

## Reporting

Each buy-back publishes metrics: volume, average price, rationale, and impact on reserves. Summary
feeds into quarterly economic reports.
