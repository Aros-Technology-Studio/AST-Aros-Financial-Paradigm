# ArosCoin Distribution Tiers

Distribution tiers tailor policy to different participant categories.

## Tiers

- **Retail**: Individuals with capped limits and simplified KYC.
- **Enterprise**: Businesses requiring higher limits and integration support.
- **Institutional**: Banks and funds with bespoke reporting obligations.
- **Public Sector**: Government or NGO programmes with dedicated oversight.

## Tier Parameters

- Maximum allocation per epoch.
- Required documentation and reporting frequency.
- Fee schedules and potential rebates.
- Lock durations and vesting rules.

## Governance

Tier policies reviewed quarterly with stakeholder feedback. Adjustments require governance approval
and supervisory confirmation.
