# ArosCoin Entry and Exit Rules

Entry and exit rules govern how ARO interacts with external assets via the Bridge Layer.

## Entry (Ingress)

- **KYC Completion**: Participants must pass identity verification before depositing.
- **Source of Funds Verification**: AML checks ensure funds originate from legitimate sources.
- **Deposit Limits**: Tiered caps based on risk profile and jurisdiction.
- **Settlement Timing**: Deposits credited after compliance clearance and blockchain confirmation.

## Exit (Egress)

- **Withdrawal Requests**: Initiated via registered channels, subject to risk scoring.
- **Holding Periods**: Minimum holding periods for large positions to prevent rapid capital flight.
- **Fiat Settlement**: Bank partners settle fiat conversions under regulatory oversight.
- **Reporting**: Exits reported to relevant regulators per jurisdictional rules.

## Controls

- **Travel Rule Compliance**: Data exchange with counterparties to satisfy regulatory requirements.
- **Suspicious Activity Monitoring**: AI models flag unusual patterns for manual review.
- **Escalation Paths**: High-risk cases escalate to compliance committee with authority to block.
