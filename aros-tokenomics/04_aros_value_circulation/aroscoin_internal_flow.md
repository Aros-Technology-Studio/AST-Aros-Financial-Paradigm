# ArosCoin Internal Flow

Internal flows describe movement of ARO between ecosystem-controlled vaults.

## Flow Categories

- **Reward Distribution**: Validator rewards and staking incentives paid from the rewards vault.
- **Operational Funding**: Treasury transfers to cover infrastructure, audits, and AI operations.
- **Grant Disbursement**: Ecosystem vault releases tokens to partners, subject to vesting controls.
- **Insurance Coverage**: Insurance vault compensates affected parties after incident verification.

## Controls

- **Approval Workflows**: Each transfer requires multi-signature approval aligned with policy.
- **Budget Tracking**: Forecasting tools compare planned vs actual flows, enabling governance oversight.
- **Exception Handling**: Deviations trigger supervisory review and potential clawbacks.

## Reporting

Internal flow reports summarise balances, transfers, and outstanding commitments each week. Data feeds
directly into financial statements and regulatory filings.
