# ArosCoin Release Schedule

The release schedule defines when and how ARO enters circulation.

## Baseline Curve

- **Year 1**: 12% of total authorised supply released with strong validator incentives.
- **Years 2-3**: Release tapers to 8% annually as ecosystem matures.
- **Year 4 onwards**: Stabilises at 4% with emphasis on organic demand.

## Adjustments

- **Performance Modifiers**: PoT metrics adjust release ±10% within policy bounds.
- **Reserve Status**: Reserve utilisation influences pacing to maintain liquidity.
- **Governance Overrides**: Council can adjust schedule with supermajority approval.

## Transparency

Schedule published as a time series with expected vs actual release. Deviations require explanatory
reports within 7 days.
