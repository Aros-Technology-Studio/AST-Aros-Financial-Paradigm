# ArosCoin Velocity Control

Velocity control keeps transaction flow aligned with economic productivity.

## Metrics

- **Transaction Count per Epoch**
- **Average Holding Period**
- **Sectoral Flow Distribution**
- **Liquidity Coverage Ratio**

## Control Mechanisms

- **Fee Modulation**: Adjust fees to temper excessive or insufficient activity.
- **Incentive Rebates**: Offer rebates for strategic sectors needing liquidity support.
- **Lock Adjustments**: Temporarily adjust lock durations to encourage or slow spending.

## Monitoring

AI telemetry analyses velocity data to detect anomalies such as wash trading or sudden drops in
productivity. Alerts feed into governance proposals for policy adjustments.
