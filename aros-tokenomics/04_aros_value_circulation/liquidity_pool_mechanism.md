# Liquidity Pool Mechanism

Liquidity pools provide continuous convertibility between ARO, fiat-backed stablecoins, and key crypto
assets.

## Pool Types

- **Primary Market Pools**: Managed by AROS to stabilise core exchange rates.
- **Partner Pools**: Operated by regulated exchanges with oversight agreements.
- **Stability Pools**: Serve as last-resort liquidity during stress events.

## Mechanics

- **Dynamic Fees**: Adjust based on utilisation to discourage runs and reward liquidity providers.
- **Reserve Targets**: Pools maintain coverage ratios; deviations trigger rebalancing or buy-backs.
- **Oracle Integration**: Price feeds from multiple sources ensure accurate valuations.

## Risk Management

- **Impermanent Loss Coverage**: Treasury funds allocate coverage for strategic partners.
- **Counterparty Risk**: Partner pools must provide collateral and submit to regular audits.
- **Stress Testing**: Scenario analysis evaluates resilience against market shocks.

## Transparency

Pool statistics—utilisation, depth, and price impact—are published in near real-time. Supervisory
alerts trigger when indicators breach thresholds.
