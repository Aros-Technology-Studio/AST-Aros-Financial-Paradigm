# Reserve Pool Policy

Reserve pools protect against liquidity shocks and ensure obligations can be met.

## Reserve Composition

- **Stable Assets**: Fiat-backed stablecoins and short-term government securities.
- **ARO Holdings**: ARO reserved for market interventions.
- **Insurance Funds**: Capital to cover slashing or operational incidents.

## Target Levels

- Maintain minimum 30% coverage of circulating supply in liquid assets.
- Hold 6 months of operating expenses in fiat equivalents.
- Maintain insurance pool sufficient for worst-case slashing scenario.

## Rebalancing

- **Periodic Rebalancing**: Monthly adjustments align reserves with targets.
- **Stress Response**: Supervisory alerts can trigger immediate reallocation.
- **Investment Policy**: Conservative investments vetted by compliance and treasury committees.

## Transparency

Quarterly disclosures summarise reserve composition, yield, and utilisation. Independent auditors
verify balances and publish attestations.
