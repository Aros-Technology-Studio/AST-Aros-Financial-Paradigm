# Value Circulation Overview

The Value Circulation layer coordinates how ARO moves between ecosystem vaults, external markets, and
stakeholders. It ensures liquidity, aligns incentives, and protects reserves.

## Objectives

- Maintain healthy liquidity across retail, enterprise, and institutional channels.
- Align supply releases with economic activity and compliance requirements.
- Provide transparent reporting on flows and reserves.

## Core Components

- **Vault Network**: Segregated vaults for validators, ecosystem funds, treasury, and insurance.
- **Liquidity Orchestrator**: Automates swaps, buy-backs, and reserve rebalancing.
- **Telemetry Engine**: Tracks velocity, reserve coverage, and market depth.
- **Reporting Hub**: Generates dashboards for stakeholders and regulators.

## Flow Types

1. **Inbound**: Conversion from fiat or crypto via the Bridge Layer into ARO.
2. **Internal**: Transfers between vaults for rewards, grants, and treasury management.
3. **Outbound**: Conversions back to fiat/crypto or settlement with partners.

## Governance Alignment

Value circulation policy is governed by the Monetary Council with feedback from AI supervisory
projections. Changes require governance approval and are reflected in the roadmap.
