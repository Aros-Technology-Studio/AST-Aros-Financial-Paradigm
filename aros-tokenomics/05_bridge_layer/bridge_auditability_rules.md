# Bridge Auditability Rules

Auditability ensures bridge operations remain transparent to regulators and partners.

## Requirements

- Maintain tamper-evident logs for all transactions and compliance events.
- Provide regulators with near real-time data feeds when required.
- Store records for statutory periods (typically 7-10 years).

## Controls

- Immutable log storage backed by decentralised transaction encoding.
- Access controls and monitoring for audit queries.
- Periodic reconciliation between on-chain records and custodial statements.

## Reporting

Quarterly reports summarise transaction volumes, compliance actions, and incidents. External auditors
validate adherence to regulations and publish assurance statements.
