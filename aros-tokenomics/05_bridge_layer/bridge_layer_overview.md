# Bridge Layer Overview

The Bridge Layer enables regulated ingress and egress between ARO and external financial systems. It
coordinates compliance, liquidity, and technology across partner networks.

## Objectives

- Provide secure cross-asset transfers while enforcing KYC/AML.
- Maintain liquidity and collateralisation for multi-network settlements.
- Offer transparent reporting to regulators and partners.

## Components

- **Compliance Gateway**: Handles identity verification, sanctions screening, and travel rule data.
- **Liquidity Router**: Directs flows across partner exchanges and internal pools.
- **Adapter Framework**: Standardises integration with blockchains, banking APIs, and payment rails.
- **Monitoring Console**: Displays real-time metrics, alerts, and audit logs.

## Governance Alignment

Bridge policy is set by governance committees in collaboration with regulatory stakeholders. Emergency
controls allow rapid suspension or rerouting when risk events occur.
