# Emergency Governance Procedures

Emergency procedures provide structured response to critical incidents.

## Triggers

- Severe security breach or network outage.
- Regulatory mandate requiring immediate action.
- Systemic market manipulation or liquidity crisis.

## Steps

1. **Alert**: Supervisory layer notifies council and committees.
2. **Assessment**: Convene emergency session to evaluate impact.
3. **Action**: Execute authorised measures (pauses, freezes, overrides).
4. **Communication**: Inform stakeholders, regulators, and public as appropriate.
5. **Review**: Post-event review documents lessons learned and follow-up actions.

## Safeguards

- Emergency actions time-limited and require ratification within 72 hours.
- Independent observers monitor process to prevent abuse.
- Audit logs capture all decisions and rationales.
