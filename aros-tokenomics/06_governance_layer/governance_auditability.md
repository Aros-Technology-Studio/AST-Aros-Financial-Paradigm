# Governance Auditability

Auditability provides transparency into governance processes.

## Artefacts

- Proposal documents and revisions.
- Committee assessments and risk analyses.
- Voting records, including anonymised voter IDs.
- Implementation logs and outcome reports.

## Storage

Artefacts stored in tamper-evident repositories with redundant backups. Key decisions mirrored into
the decentralised transaction encoding ledger for public verification.

## Access

- Public portal for non-sensitive records.
- Restricted portal for regulators and auditors with additional detail.
- Access logs reviewed monthly to detect misuse.
