# Governance Layer Overview

The Governance Layer defines how strategic and operational decisions are proposed, evaluated, and
implemented across AST.

## Structure

- **Council**: Executive body with authority over monetary and emergency actions.
- **Assembly**: Wider stakeholder forum with advisory votes.
- **Committees**: Specialized groups (Compliance, Technology, Risk) providing analysis.

## Process

1. **Proposal Drafting**: Submitters provide rationale, quantitative analysis, and expected impact.
2. **Review**: Committees assess compliance, risk, and economic implications.
3. **Voting**: Weighted voting with stake and compliance adjustments.
4. **Execution**: Approved proposals go through time-locked implementation with audit logging.

## Transparency

Governance decisions published with meeting minutes, vote breakdowns, and follow-up tasks. Disputes
can be escalated to arbitration panels formed from supervisory representatives.
