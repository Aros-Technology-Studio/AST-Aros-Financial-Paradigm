# Governance Roles and Permissions

Roles define responsibilities and access levels within governance operations.

## Roles

- **Council Member**: Full voting rights, proposal sponsorship, emergency powers.
- **Committee Member**: Review and advisory authority within domain.
- **Community Delegate**: Vote on behalf of a constituency with limited proposal rights.
- **Observer**: Access to information without voting capability.

## Permissions Matrix

| Role | Submit Proposal | Vote | Emergency Action | Access Sensitive Data |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Council Member | Yes | Yes | Yes (with quorum) | Yes |
| Committee Member | Advisory | Optional | No | Scoped |
| Community Delegate | Yes (with sponsor) | Yes | No | Limited |
| Observer | No | No | No | Public only |

## Onboarding & Offboarding

All role changes require governance approval and are logged. Background checks and training ensure
role holders understand obligations.
