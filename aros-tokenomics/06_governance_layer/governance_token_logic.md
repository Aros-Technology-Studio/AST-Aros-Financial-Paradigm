# Governance Token Logic

Governance tokens represent participation rights separate from circulating ARO when necessary.

## Purpose

- Enable differentiated voting rights for strategic stakeholders.
- Provide non-transferable credentials for regulatory bodies.
- Support quadratic or conviction-based voting experiments.

## Implementation

- Minted as non-transferable tokens tied to identity credentials.
- Expire or refresh periodically to reflect current eligibility.
- Managed by smart contracts controlled by the governance council.

## Safeguards

- Revocation procedures for misconduct.
- Audit trails linking tokens to compliance records.
- Privacy-preserving attestations to protect sensitive identities.
