# Quorum Validation Rules

Quorum ensures adequate participation before proposals can pass.

## Thresholds

- **Standard Proposals**: Minimum 40% of eligible weighted votes.
- **High Impact (monetary, security)**: Minimum 60% and supervisory sign-off.
- **Emergency Actions**: Fast-track with 30% but require post-event ratification.

## Validation

- Automated scripts verify quorum after voting window closes.
- Manual oversight ensures compliance adjustments correctly applied.
- Results recorded with cryptographic proofs in audit logs.

## Failure Handling

Proposals failing quorum can be resubmitted with adjustments. Repeated failures prompt review of
participation incentives.
