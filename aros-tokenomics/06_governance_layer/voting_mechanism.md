# Voting Mechanism

Voting determines whether proposals are adopted.

## Eligibility

- Stakeholders holding KYC-cleared ARO.
- Delegated representatives from institutions.
- Supervisory observers with veto authority but no vote count.

## Weighting

- Base weight proportional to stake held.
- Compliance multiplier adjusts weight for entities meeting reporting standards.
- Reputation modifier from AI agents rewards consistent constructive participation.

## Phases

1. **Snapshot**: Record eligible voters and balances at snapshot time.
2. **Deliberation**: Open forum for discussion and Q&A.
3. **Voting Window**: Typically 72 hours with transparent tally updates.
4. **Finalisation**: Results published with breakdown and quorum verification.

## Security

- Votes signed with hardware-backed keys.
- Anti-bribery measures via monitoring suspicious transfers.
- On-chain proof ensures immutability.
