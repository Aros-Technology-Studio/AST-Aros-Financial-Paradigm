# Emission Reporting and Traceability

Reporting ensures stakeholders can verify emission activity.

## Deliverables

- Epoch reports with input metrics, adjustments, and final allocations.
- Monthly summaries comparing actual vs planned emission.
- Machine-readable datasets for auditors and analysts.

## Traceability

Each emission event linked to underlying data and approvals. Hashes recorded in the audit ledger for
public verification.
