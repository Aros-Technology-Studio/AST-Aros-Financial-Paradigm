# Emission Rollbacks and Freeze Rules

Rollback and freeze procedures protect against erroneous or fraudulent emission.

## Rollback Criteria

- Proven data corruption in input metrics.
- Unauthorised override approval.
- System fault causing miscalculation.

## Freeze Criteria

- Regulatory directive.
- Supervisory risk alert reaching critical level.
- Chain security incident requiring pause.

## Procedure

Freeze halts new emission while preserving state. Rollback reverts minted tokens via coordination with
Token Management Layer and publishes remediation report.
