# Emission Trigger Conditions

Triggers define when emission cycles execute.

## Scheduled Triggers

- Regular epochs every 15 minutes.
- Monthly macro review adjusting coefficients.

## Dynamic Triggers

- Proof of Transaction thresholds reached.
- Governance-approved special releases.
- Emergency liquidity interventions.

## Safeguards

Each trigger cross-checked against policy registry to prevent double minting. Supervisory veto can halt
triggers if risk indicators elevated.
