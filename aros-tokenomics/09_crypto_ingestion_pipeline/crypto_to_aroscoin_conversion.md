# Crypto to ArosCoin Conversion

Conversion swaps incoming crypto assets to ARO at policy-compliant rates.

## Pricing

- Aggregated price feeds from exchanges and OTC partners.
- Spread adjustments for risk and liquidity.
- Fees partially burned or allocated to treasury.

## Settlement

Converted ARO distributed per user instructions with lock periods based on risk profile. Fiat hedging
strategies employed to maintain reserve ratios.
