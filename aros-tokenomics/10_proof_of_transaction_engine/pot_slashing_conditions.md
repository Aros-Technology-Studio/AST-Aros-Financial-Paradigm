# PoT Slashing Conditions

Slashing deters malicious behaviour and compensates affected parties.

## Conditions

- Double-signing or conflicting attestations.
- Proven censorship of legitimate transactions.
- Collusion to manipulate PoT scores.
- Failure to respond to challenges within mandated timeframe.

## Penalties

- Loss of staked ARO and collateral.
- Temporary or permanent ban from NodeChain.
- Redistribution of slashed funds to affected users and insurance vault.

## Appeals

Validators may appeal via governance channels. Appeals require new evidence and undergo transparent
review.
