# Slashing and Penalty Rules

Rules define when stakes are penalised and how funds are redistributed.

## Infractions

- Downtime beyond tolerance thresholds.
- Security breaches or key compromise.
- Fraudulent behaviour or collusion.

## Penalties

- Partial or full stake slashing depending on severity.
- Redistribution to insurance vault and affected parties.
- Temporary suspension or permanent removal from validator set.

## Appeals

Validators may appeal; successful appeals restore slashed funds if misconduct disproven.
