# Stake Freeze and Unlock Rules

Rules define how staked assets can be frozen or unlocked.

## Freeze Conditions

- Pending investigation for misconduct.
- Non-compliance with reporting obligations.
- Security incidents affecting validator nodes.

## Unlock Process

- Standard unlock requires unbonding period (14 days).
- Emergency unlock may be granted by governance for justified cases.
- Frozen stake remains locked until investigation concludes.
