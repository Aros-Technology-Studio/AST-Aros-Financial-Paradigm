# Staking Overview

Staking secures the network by requiring validators to lock ARO and adhere to performance standards.

## Objectives

- Align incentives with network reliability.
- Provide collateral for slashing and penalties.
- Reward long-term participation through yield.

## Components

- **Staking Contracts**: Manage deposits, withdrawals, and rewards.
- **Compliance Checks**: Ensure stakers meet regulatory requirements.
- **Monitoring**: Track performance and trigger slashing when necessary.
