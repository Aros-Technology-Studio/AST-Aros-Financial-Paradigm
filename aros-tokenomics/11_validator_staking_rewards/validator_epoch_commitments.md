# Validator Epoch Commitments

Epoch commitments describe obligations validators accept each epoch.

## Commitments

- Maintain minimum uptime and latency targets.
- Submit attestations within defined windows.
- Participate in governance reviews and audits.
- Provide telemetry for AI monitoring.

## Enforcement

Failure to meet commitments results in reward reduction or slashing depending on severity. Repeated
breaches trigger suspension.
