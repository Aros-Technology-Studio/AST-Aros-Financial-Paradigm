# AI Governance Escalation

Escalation protocols define how AI-driven concerns reach governance bodies.

## Triggers

- Critical anomaly score exceeding thresholds.
- Model behaviour drift detected.
- Conflicts between AI recommendations and human judgement.

## Workflow

Alerts routed to AI oversight committee, which convenes cross-functional team, assesses impact, and
recommends actions to governance council. All escalations documented with outcomes.
