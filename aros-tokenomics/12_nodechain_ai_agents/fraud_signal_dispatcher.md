# Fraud Signal Dispatcher

Dispatcher routes fraud alerts to operational teams.

## Workflow

1. Receive signals from anomaly and pattern recognition models.
2. Prioritise based on risk, value, and regulatory impact.
3. Dispatch tickets to compliance, security, or governance teams.
4. Track resolution and feed outcomes into model retraining.

## Integration

Integrates with case management systems and supports API/webhook notifications for partner agencies.
