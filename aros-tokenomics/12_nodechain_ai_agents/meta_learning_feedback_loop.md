# Meta-Learning Feedback Loop

Feedback loop ensures AI models evolve responsibly.

## Process

1. Collect labelled outcomes from fraud cases, governance decisions, and user feedback.
2. Evaluate model performance metrics.
3. Retrain models in isolated environment with reproducibility controls.
4. Deploy updates after governance review and monitoring period.

## Governance

Ethics committee oversees data usage, bias testing, and approval of new models.
