# Implementation Guide

Guide describes steps to deploy the supervisory layer.

## Steps

1. Provision secure infrastructure separated from operational networks.
2. Deploy data ingestion pipelines with access controls.
3. Configure analytics and decision engines with policy parameters.
4. Integrate communication channels with governance and regulators.
5. Conduct testing, including incident simulations and failovers.

## Maintenance

Regularly review models, update policies, and audit access logs.
