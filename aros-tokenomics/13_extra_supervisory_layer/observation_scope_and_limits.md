# Observation Scope and Limits

Defines what the supervisory layer can observe and boundaries to protect privacy.

## Scope

- Technical telemetry (performance, faults).
- Economic indicators (liquidity, emission, reserves).
- Compliance status (KYC/AML events, investigations).

## Limits

- Personal data minimised and access restricted.
- Observability subject to legal agreements with partners.
- Oversight board reviews data usage to prevent overreach.
