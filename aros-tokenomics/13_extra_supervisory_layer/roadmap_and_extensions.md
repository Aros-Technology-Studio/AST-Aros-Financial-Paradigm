# Roadmap and Extensions

Roadmap describes planned enhancements for supervisory layer.

## Near-Term

- Integrate predictive maintenance models for validator hardware.
- Expand observer node network to additional regulators.

## Mid-Term

- Introduce privacy-preserving analytics for cross-border cooperation.
- Automate policy simulations with interactive dashboards.

## Long-Term

- Explore integration with external ESG reporting frameworks.
- Develop shared supervisory consortium with partner networks.
