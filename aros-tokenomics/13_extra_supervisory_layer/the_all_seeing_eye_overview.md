# The All-Seeing Eye Overview

The All-Seeing Eye is the supervisory meta-layer overseeing all other components.

## Objectives

- Provide holistic situational awareness across technical, economic, and compliance domains.
- Coordinate emergency responses and escalation workflows.
- Deliver transparency to regulators and stakeholders.

## Components

- **Observation Grid**: Aggregates multi-layer telemetry.
- **Decision Engine**: Applies policies and generates recommendations.
- **Communication Hub**: Interfaces with governance, regulators, and partners.
