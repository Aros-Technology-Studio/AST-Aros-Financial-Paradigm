# Use Cases and Examples

Examples illustrate supervisory interventions.

## Examples

- **Liquidity Stress Response**: Detection of liquidity shortfall triggers amber signal, leading to
bridge throttling and treasury injection.
- **Validator Misconduct**: Behaviour monitor flags abnormal latency; investigation confirms double-
signing, resulting in slashing and governance review.
- **Regulatory Alert**: External regulator requests report; observer nodes provide dataset and
Supervisory team oversees disclosure.
