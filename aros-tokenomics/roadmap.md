# Roadmap

The roadmap structures delivery of the AST Aros Financial Paradigm into phased milestones aligned with regulatory approvals and
technical readiness.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Q4 2024)
- Finalize economic simulation baselines and emission policies.
- Deploy PoT validator testnet with shadow staking rewards.
- Complete compliance bridge prototype integrations with partner banks.

## Phase 2 — Controlled Launch (Q1 2025)
- Enable limited-market ARO circulation with capped liquidity vaults.
- Integrate AI agents for validator behaviour monitoring and fraud detection.
- Achieve regulatory sign-off for fiat on/off-ramps and data residency requirements.

## Phase 3 — Expansion (Q2 2025)
- Roll out multi-network bridge adapters and cross-border settlement pilots.
- Launch All-Seeing Eye supervisory layer with anomaly escalation workflows.
- Extend governance participation to institutional stakeholders with tiered voting.

## Phase 4 — Optimization (Q3 2025)
- Introduce decentralized transaction encoding for immutable audit trails.
- Optimize staking reward formulas based on live performance metrics.
- Publish public transparency dashboards and quarterly compliance reports.
