# Roadmap

Milestones guiding delivery of the AST Aros Financial Paradigm.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Q4 2024)
- Finalise emission policy, volatility controls, and reserve targets.
- Deploy PoT validator testnet with shadow staking and telemetry exports.
- Complete compliance bridge prototype integrations with partner banks.
- Publish baseline threat model and operational playbooks.

## Phase 2 — Controlled Launch (Q1 2025)
- Enable limited-market ARO circulation with capped liquidity vaults.
- Integrate AI agents for validator monitoring, fraud detection, and supervisory reporting.
- Achieve regulatory sign-off for fiat on/off-ramps and data residency agreements.
- Launch transparency dashboards for validators, partners, and regulators.

## Phase 3 — Expansion (Q2 2025)
- Roll out multi-network bridge adapters and cross-border settlement pilots.
- Launch All-Seeing Eye supervisory layer with anomaly escalation workflows.
- Extend governance participation to institutional stakeholders with tiered voting.
- Open ecosystem grant programmes linked to milestone-based vesting.

## Phase 4 — Optimisation (Q3 2025)
- Introduce decentralised transaction encoding for immutable audit trails.
- Optimise staking reward formulas and emission coefficients using live telemetry.
- Publish quarterly compliance and sustainability reports with independent assurance.
- Prepare groundwork for consortium oversight and ESG reporting integration.
